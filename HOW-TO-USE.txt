HOW TO USE
================================================================================

1. Double-click "assignment-tracker.html" to open it in your web browser
   (Works in Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge - any modern browser)

2. You'll see a table with all 95 assignments

3. CLICK THE CHECKBOX next to any assignment to mark it complete
   - The row turns GREEN automatically!
   - Your checkmarks are SAVED in your browser (they'll still be there when you reopen)

4. Use the filters at the top to:
   - Show only one course
   - Show only a specific week  
   - Show only pending or completed assignments
   - Search for specific assignments

5. The stats at the top show your progress for each course

================================================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Q: My checkboxes disappeared!
A: If you cleared your browser cache/cookies, the checkmarks are gone.
   They're stored in your browser's localStorage.
   Bookmark the page and don't clear your browser data.

Q: Can I use this on my phone?
A: Yes! It's fully responsive and works great on mobile.

Q: Can I share this with classmates?
A: Yes, just send them the assignment-tracker.html file.
   Each person's checkmarks are saved separately in their own browser.
